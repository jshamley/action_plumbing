Who we are

Dick Zastrow - Owner and Master Plumber

Dick Zastrow is President of Action Plumbing & Heating Inc. Dick started in the plumbing trade in 1972 in Nebraska. In 1982 he received his Master Plumber's license in Nebraska. In 1983 he and his family moved to Colorado. He received his Journeyman's and Master's license in Colorado in 1984.

Action Plumbing & Heating Inc was started in September 1993 as an S Corporation after Dick's partnership with another Loveland plumbing company for eight years. Dick started with one employee in 1993 and has worked his way up to eight employees now.

Due to the amount of repair work we do, we know what works and what doesn't. Our goal is to use quality products and install them in a professional manner to ensure years of trouble free service. Every employee of Action Plumbing and Heating is trained well and we do the job right the first time to avoid any unnecessary hassle of having to fix avoidable problems later.

Safety is important to Action Plumbing & Heating. We hold monthly safety meetings and jobsite inspections to help train our technicians as well as maintaining a drug free workplace.
